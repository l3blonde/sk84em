About Us

Longboarding is more than just a sport or a hobby — it's a lifestyle, a form of self-expression, and above all, a community. SK84EM is a longboarding community at Surfer's Paradise in Knokke, Belgium that promotes eco-friendly lifestyle without rules and boundaries. SK84EM offers e-longboards with buil-in GoPro 360 degree swivel camera and Smart Ring to share live-stream videos. SK84EM is a mobile application that allows users to stream live video and share longboarding experience in real time.

our mission

push the limits of longboarding to a greener future

A central question of our time is how we transition our society to clean energy fast enough to confront the planet's environmental crisis. The short answer is designing new technologies and implementing eco-friendly lifestyle over the next decade.

Eco-friendly transport vehicles and sustainable life-style can reduce emissions at speed and scale, save money, create new jobs, and improve public health. Clean energy now costs less than fossil fuels, making it cheaper to save the Earth than to destroy it, and the mission SK84EM has undertaken.

This goal is now within reach : to make SK84EM's e-longboard a serious alterbative transport for your shorter city trips.

By partnering with experienced organizations like Go Forest, who is focused on restoring forests worldwide, SK84EM aims to contribute in securing a safe climate future. Thus, we plant a tree for every e-longboard we sell and developing innovative technology for our e-longboard.
