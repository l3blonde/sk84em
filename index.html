WE'RE ON A MISSION

To promote longboarding lifestyle.
Without rules and boundarie.

WITH A VISION

To push the limits of longboarding to a more responsible future.

A longboard is a wooden piece of art, so SK84EM plants a tree for every board we sell.

SK84EM has partnered with a Belgian organisation, named Go Forest, who is focused on restoring forests worldwide.

One planet, one longboard, one tree. Let's plant trees so we can continue to make longboards in a sustainable way!

POWERED BY A GOAL

To make e-longboard a viable alternative for your city trips.

At SK84EM, we’re committed to develop the revolutionary Smart IoT technology to enable our e-longboards to intelligently adjust the power model and electric board features making it very easy to use even for noob riders.

Our e-wheels can be easily used in both off-road and on-road conditions. And with GoPro E-RING you can even record videos of your longboarding experience while sharing it to your friends on instagram.
